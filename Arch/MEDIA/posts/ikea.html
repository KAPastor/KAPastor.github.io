How to install an Ikea kitchen cooktop

A simple problem that should show up on internet search.

Posted by KAPastor on January 2nd, 2017

So I am currently renovating my kitchen and we have decided to go with the whole Ikea setup. Things are going good, however installing the cooktop was super confustion. As it turns out, you MUST cut the crap out of your cabinet in order to fit the standard Ikea cooktop into the standard Ikea cabinet. For reference we are using the 30 inch oven base cabinet with a 30 inch 5 element NUTID cooktop. I went to Ikea and asked some questions and they were nice enough to show me how they did it. I am posting the photos and descriptions here so hopefully an Ikea trip will be saved by.

What Ikea Does

They cut the whole damn sides off. Here are some pictures

Showing the cuts on the wall side of the cabinet. Showing the cuts on the front side of the cabinet.

What I Did

While what Ikea does is all good, I wanted to make my cuts a bit more clean. When you read the cooktop installation instructions they don't actually require a full cutout. Therfore I took a router to it and carved half the walls.

The carveout with the router. Much good. Very yes.
